# Session Log: 2026-06-23 -- CA Education Lab theme (official pptx port)

**Status:** IN PROGRESS

## Objective
Replace the old unofficial CA Ed Lab theme with a faithful LaTeX port of the
official CEL PowerPoint template, modular across the native 4:3 AND 16:9 aspect
ratios (toggled via the beamer `aspectratio` option). pdfLaTeX + Carlito (free
Calibri-metric clone), all spacing derived from `\paperwidth`/`\paperheight`.

## Key facts (from the pptx)
- Native 4:3 (10in x 7.5in). Navy `#014B84`, gold `#DFB305`. Fonts Calibri / Calibri Light.
- Title slide: navy field over a white logo strip, gold rule on the boundary.
- Content: navy header band, 40pt title, gold rule full-width under the band, slide number top-right.
- Section divider (slide 4): solid navy bg + FOUR gold lines that cross at the corners and run to the slide edges; centered white title.
- Final slide: "Thank you!" + collaborators + acknowledgement note (centered).

## Incremental Work Log
- Ported the 4 caedlab `.sty` files; shared body in `caedlab_theme_content.tex`,
  driven by `caedlab_theme_test.tex` (4:3) and `caedlab_theme_test_169.tex` (16:9).
- Added TOC (gold numbered circles), `\AtBeginSection` divider, full-bleed gold
  rule under the frame-title band, official acknowledgement placeholder (centered),
  navy+gold section divider, and the final "Thank you!" slide.

## Tweak round (user feedback, this session)
- **Title-slide gold rule thicker:** introduced a dedicated `\celtitlerule` (4pt)
  in the master, separate from the header band's thin `\celaccent` (2pt). Inner
  theme's title-slide divider now draws with `\celtitlerule`.
- **Bottom white logo strip taller:** `\celstrip` 0.19 -> 0.22 `\paperheight`.
- **Section divider gold rules cross & reach edges:** replaced the inset
  `rectangle` `\draw` with four full-span lines (horizontals x=0->\paperwidth at
  y=`\celborderinset` and `\paperheight-\celborderinset`; verticals y=0->\paperheight
  at x=`\celborderinset` and `\paperwidth-\celborderinset`). They cross at corners
  and extend to the borders, matching pptx slide 4.

## Tweak round 2 (user feedback, this session)
- **TOC circles gold:** `section in toc` circle `fill=celNavy` -> `fill=celGold`; numeral
  recolored white -> `celNavy` for legibility on the lighter gold.
- **Section-divider titles proper-cased:** wrapped the divider title in titlecaps'
  `\titlecap`. `\insertsection` expands to a `\hyperlink` wrapper (verified via
  `\detokenize` probe), so titlecaps leaked navigation tokens ("navigation6..."). Fixed
  by intercepting `\section` with `\RenewDocumentCommand{...}{s o m}` to stash the raw
  title in `\celcursection`, then `\titlecap{\celcursection}`. Added `\RequirePackage{titlecaps}`.
  Caveat: the rare `\section<overlay>` form is not preserved by the wrapper.

## Tweak round 3 (user feedback, this session)
- **Final "Thank you!" slide restructured to match pptx slide5.** Inspected the pptx
  (Rectangle 2): a single left-aligned, top-anchored block grouped by organization —
  org name as a heading (21pt), member names beneath (default size), blank line between
  groups, funding note (12pt) at the bottom. Replaced the prior `\item Name --- Org`
  itemize with stacked groups: org headers `\large\color{celNavy}` (navy = on-brand
  interpretation; pptx left them default color, only larger), members normal black,
  `[t]` top-aligned frame, `\vfill` before the acknowledgement note.

## Tweak round 4 (user feedback, this session)
- **TOC circle numeral back to white** (`\textcolor{celNavy}` -> `white`) on the gold circle.
- **Title-page acknowledgement centering fixed (was right-pushed in 4:3).** Root cause: the
  minipage width was `\paperwidth-2\celmargin` (~0.9 paperwidth), wider than the body
  `\textwidth`, so the box overflowed the column flush-left -> visual center right of
  slide-center (worst in the narrower 4:3 column). Changed width to `\textwidth-2\celmargin`
  so it fits the column and centers symmetrically at both ratios.
